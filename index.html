Introduction

For the majority of the lifts, you should alternate them per workout. For example, if you do the floor press on Monday, do the overhead press on Wednesday, and then the floor press on Friday. You'll want to do one exercise from each section per day, so that you get a full body workout. Don't forget to rest at least one day inbetween workouts.

Program

3 sets of 5 reps Dumbell Floor Press Overhead Press
3 sets of 5 reps Single Arm Row Chinups
3 sets of 5 reps Goblet Squats Romanian Deadlifts
3 sets of 8 to 12 reps Hip Thrust More Hip Thrusts
3 sets of 8 to 12 reps Dumbbell Lying Shoulder External Rotation Same as before

Tips

  • For the 3x5 sets, your last set is as many reps as possible. Try to always get more than 5.
  • For the 3x5 sets, rest 3 to 5 minutes between them.
  • Increase weight by the smallest amount per workout.
  • For the 3x8-12 sets, rest 90 seconds to 120 seconds.
  • Warmup for 5 minutes on your treadmill
  • Stretch your hip flexors, ankle, and chest every day.
  • When you get a chinup bar, do dead hangs as well. Hang for short periods of time at first. Build up your time.
  • Never bend forward on the rows and squats/deadlifts. Gotta keep that core tight.
